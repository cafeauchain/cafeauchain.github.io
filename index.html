About

Cafe Au Chain wants to encourage conversation about coffee. Whether you're a roaster, coffee trader, or coffee producer, we want to hear your story. We are a startup from Athens, GA, a team of coffee enthusiasts and software developers ready to bring the coffee industry into the digital age.
